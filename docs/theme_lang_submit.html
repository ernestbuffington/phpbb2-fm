Submit for Official Release

Once your new language/theme is complete and you have thoroughly tested it on your board, in the community spirit you should submit it for official release through our site so other users can also enjoy it.

Zip up the complete language/theme directory (don't forget the theme images/ and the spellchecker file for new languages!) with either 7-Zip, WinZip, or WinRar. Attach and send it via Private Message or E-mail, once approved it will be official released on the Fully Modded phpBB site.
